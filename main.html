Summary

Inception is a science fiction thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. It follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who has the rare ability to enter people's dreams and steal secrets from their subconscious. Tasked with performing the impossible – planting an idea into someone's mind – Cobb and his team navigate through multiple layers of dreams in a high-stakes mission.
